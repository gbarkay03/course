Alessandro Mahmoud

Born 12 September 1992, known professionally as Mahmood, is an Italian singer-songwriter.

About the Legend

☛ Born in Milan to an Egyptian father and a Sardinian mother from Orosei, Mahmood was raised around Gratosoglio, a district of Milan .

☛ He has won the Sanremo Music Festival twice, in 2019 with the song "Soldi" and in 2022 alongside Blanco with the song "Brividi". .

☛ Mahmood has released two studio albums, Gioventù bruciata and Ghettolimpo, both of which reached the top two of the Italian albums chart. Amazon Music Italy enlisted him as one of the most streamed artists in 2021 with Madame, Cos Mass and Alessandra Amoroso in Italy .
